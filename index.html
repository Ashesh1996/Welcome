#HungerSaver

PURPOSE

We will reduce the food wastage using our web application, where the users didn’t require calling or contacting or filling up details to any particular NGO. In our online portal the users will be able to signing up to be a food donor. They can posts the details regarding the quantity of food or may request for food, to donate. Based on the user’s location, our web application will automatically send notification message to the nearest NGO. Even the users get notified when organization such as food bank or any NGO need donors. The organizations can also signup to create post and events that quickly notify donors nearby. We will also analyze the user and organizations data and retrieve an analyzed results regarding percentage of food wastage within an area which not only aware people from food wastage, but provides the success rate feeding poor hungry people.
